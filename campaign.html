Design Club Initiative

Bridging Design and Technology

As part of our vision to foster innovation and create well-rounded professionals, we plan to launch a dedicated design club. This initiative aims to:

  • Provide a platform for students to explore the intersection of design and technology
  • Organize workshops and seminars on UI/UX design, graphic design, and design thinking
  • Collaborate with tech projects to ensure user-centric and aesthetically pleasing outcomes
  • Host design challenges and hackathons to solve real-world problems
  • Invite industry experts to share insights on the importance of design in tech

By bridging the gap between design and technology, we aim to produce innovators who can create not just functional, but also intuitive and beautiful solutions.
